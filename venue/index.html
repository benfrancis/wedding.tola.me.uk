Our marriage ceremony and wedding reception are taking place at South Farm in Cambridgeshire. South Farm is a wedding venue and working farm on the Wimpole Hall estate which dates back to the Tudor times of the 16th Century. The farm was bought as a ruin in the 1970s and the house and barns, in 20 acres of grounds, were lovingly restored and turned into a family home and eco-friendly family business where much of the food is grown on site.

You can find out more information about South Farm on their website.
